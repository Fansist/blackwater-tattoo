Good To Know

Frequently Asked

Straight answers before you sit down in the chair.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are welcome at Blackwater Tattoo, though appointments are preferred for larger custom pieces so an artist can plan the design and set aside proper time. Call or stop by to check current availability.

What tattoo styles do the artists specialize in?

The shop covers a wide range: black & gray realism, portrait work, Japanese-influenced designs, American traditional, biomechanical, and tribal. Bring reference images and talk through your idea with an artist before booking.

How do I prepare for a tattoo appointment?

General best practice at any tattoo studio: get a good night's sleep, eat beforehand, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours prior. Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed.

Is there a related Blackwater Tattoo location?

Yes, there's a related location at 323 Broadway in Chula Vista, the original shop opened by owner Mike “SouthBay Mike” Kellerman back in 1996.

How should I take care of a new tattoo?

Your artist will walk you through aftercare before you leave the chair, typically keeping the area clean, moisturized, and out of direct sun while it heals. Ask in-shop for their specific aftercare instructions.
